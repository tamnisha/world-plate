World Plate?

World PlateWorld Plate offers a one-of-a-kind dining experience that takes guests on a culinary adventure right in their own town. Every week, diners can choose up to five different countries to highlight their meals over the next seven days. With real recipes, skilled chefs, and lively settings that showcase each culture, World Plate turns regular dining into an exciting celebration of global food diversity.!
